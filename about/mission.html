About Us

We are The Imvelo Foundation. We are a non-profit organisation that aims to assist and uplift rural communities with the support of environmental projects. We help were we can, through verious Projects and other philanthropic endevours.

Volunteers

We run a volunteer program, in conjunction with top educational institutions from across the globe. We give students pursuing an undergraduate education an opportunity where they can learn and apply the skills they have gained during their studies to real world scenarios.

On The Near Horizon

We are currently in the process of securing accreditation from two prestigiouse organisations: Charity Navigator and the ECFA. We do this in hopes of convaying ust how serious we are, as an organization, to helping those that need it most.
